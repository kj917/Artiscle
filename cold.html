Artist Around The World

Coldplay’s 2025 Music of the Spheres World Tour

A Planetary Spectacle

  1. Asia & Middle East (Early 2025)
    • Abu Dhabi – Kicked off early January with multiple performances at the Zayed Sports City Stadium.
    • Mumbai – January 18–19 saw Coldplay at Mumbai’s DY Patil Stadium, supported by Elyanna.
    • Hong Kong – April 9, 11, and 12 at Kai Tak Stadium marked the band’s first return since 2009.
    • Seoul – Four sold-out shows (April 16–25) at Goyang Stadium delivered powerful emotional moments.
  2. North America (May–July 2025)
    • The North American leg included a string of stadium shows from May 31 in Stanford, June stops in Las Vegas, Denver, and El Paso, and July run through Toronto, Foxborough, Madison, Nashville, and Miami.
    • These also featured affordable Infinity Tickets — pairs of seats priced at $20 each — aimed at making concerts more accessible.
  3. UK & Europe Finale (August–September 2025)
    • Shows wrapped up with performances in Hull (August 18–19) and an impressive ten-night run at Wembley Stadium (August 22–September 8).
    • 10% of proceeds from UK shows are being donated to the Music Venue Trust, supporting grassroots music venues.
