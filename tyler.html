  • Big Poe - Features Pharrell Williams and introduces the album with a playful, aggressive energy and altered persona.

A Shift Toward Pure Groove

Unlike his introspective and concept-driven previous release, Chromakopia (2024), this project leans heavily into dance explosion—electro, synth-funk, disco, Miami bass, and house—embracing sound and energy over deep narrative. Tyler flips the script, inviting listeners to forget introspection and just move: “body movement / no sitting still.”
